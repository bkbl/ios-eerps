Overview

General Information

  • Animations of transitions are recorded in 60 frames per second and this is a base
  • Animation lenght is described as [no of frames / 60] fe. 0.5 sec = [30/60]
  • All animations are eased "ease-in - ease-out" to give more organic feeling
  • Animations are shown on iPhone 6 - 375pt x 667pt Canvas but timings remaing the same for other devices
  • Back transitions triggered by "back" button on the navbar are instant
  • Popup / overlay close transitions are played 2x speed of an opening transitions
  • Color transformations from one to another are in hex codes fe. white to black [#FFFFFF to #000000]
  • Opacity transformations in % of opacity, opaque to transparent [100% to 0%]

How to use assets

  • Below is the section of a single transition
  • You can download high quality videos of transitions by clicking Download HQ Video
  • Click Download Animation Timings for high quality videos of transitions with time markers per each new subanimation. Analyse it frame by frame
  • To do it open *.mov files in QuickTime Player and move left-right arrows
  • 00060 = 1 sec
  • ALWAYS refer to Timings videos for animation analysis and reference
